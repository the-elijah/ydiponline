location_on Remote / Hybrid  |  Full-time

Job Description

We are looking for a talented and passionate UX Designer to join our creative team. You will be responsible for designing intuitive, accessible, and engaging user experiences for our clients' digital products. You will work closely with product managers, developers, and other designers to bring ideas to life.

Key Responsibilities

  • check Conduct user research and usability testing to understand user needs.
  • check Create user personas, journey maps, wireframes, and prototypes.
  • check Design beautiful and functional user interfaces (UI).
  • check Collaborate with the development team to ensure design fidelity.

Requirements

  • check 3+ years of experience in UX/UI design.
  • check Proficiency in Figma, Adobe XD, or Sketch.
  • check Strong understanding of user-centered design principles.
  • check Excellent communication and teamwork skills.

Job Summary

  • Department: Design
  • Experience: Mid-Senior Level
  • Salary: Competitive
